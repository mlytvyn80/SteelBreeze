00094 /*
00095   1995 IERS Annual Report (1996)
00096   page V-3 says:
00097   The data on Earth rotation and reference frames include series of the 
00098   Earth Orientation Parameters (EOP), Sets of Station Coordinates (SSC),
00099   and Radio Source Coordinates (RSC). They include also series of the 
00100   Atmospheric Angular Momentum (AAM). The data sets are labelled as folows.
00101   
00102   EOP(AAAAA)XX E YY             for Earth Orientation Parameters
00103   SSC(AAAAA)XX E YY             for Sets of Station Coordinates
00104   SSV(AAAAA)XX E YY             for Sets of Station Velocities
00105   RSC(AAAAA)XX E YY             for Radio Source Coordinates
00106   AAM(AAAAA)XX E YY             for Atmospheric Angular Momentum
00107 
00108 enum SolutionType {TYPE_EOP, TYPE_SSC, TYPE_SSV, TYPE_RSC, TYPE_AAM, TYPE_UNKN};
00109 
00110   where AAAAA= acronym of the institute producing the results 
00111   (one to five letters)
00112   
00113   XX= year-1990 when the data set was computed
00114   E= the observing technique
00115   
00116   R:    Radio interferometry
00117   A:    optical astrometry
00118   M:    Lunar laser ranging
00119   P:    Global Positioning System
00120   L:    satellite laser ranging
00121   D:    satellite Doppler tracking
00122   C:    conventional geodesy (for SSC)
00123   *:    atmospheric data
00124   */
